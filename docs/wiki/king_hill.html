King of the Hill

Two-team competitive environment featuring knights and archers, destructible walls, control point capture, and team-shared rewards. The most complex MAPOX environment.

Game Rules

Teams

Agents are split evenly into Red and Blue teams. Red spawns along the bottom edge, Blue along the top edge.

Agent Types

At reset, each agent is randomly assigned one of two types (mirrored for both teams):

TypeTileHealthPrimary Action
KnightAGENT_KNIGHT (8)2 HPMelee attack (1 tile ahead in facing direction)
ArcherAGENT_ARCHER (9)1 HPShoot arrow (projectile in facing direction)

Actions Used

All 7 actions are available:

IDActionEffect
0–3MoveMove in cardinal direction and update facing
4StayHold position (keeps current facing)
5PrimaryKnight: melee attack / Archer: shoot arrow
6DigDestroy a destructible wall in facing direction

Movement

  • All agents propose moves simultaneously. A random permutation resolves conflicts.
  • An agent cannot move to a tile occupied by a wall, destructible wall, or another agent (first-come in random order wins).
  • Moving updates the agent's facing direction.

Combat

Knights (Melee)

  • Primary action deals 1 damage to the tile directly ahead (in facing direction).
  • After attacking, the knight enters a melee_timeout (default 3 steps) during which it can only stay.

Archers (Ranged)

  • Primary action spawns an arrow projectile on the tile ahead.
  • Arrows move 1 tile per step in the direction they were fired.
  • Arrows deal 1 damage to any agent on the tiles they pass through (current position and destination).
  • Arrows are destroyed when they hit a wall or destructible wall, or after arrow_timeout steps (default 6).
  • After firing, the archer enters a cooldown of arrow_timeout steps before it can fire again.

Death & Respawn

  • When an agent's health reaches 0, it immediately respawns at its starting position with full health.

Digging

  • The dig action destroys a destructible wall in the facing direction.
  • After digging, the agent enters a dig_timeout (default 5 steps).

Control Points

  • num_flags control points are placed in the center band of the map.
  • A control point is captured by the last team to step on it (Red=1, Blue=2, Neutral=0).
  • Each step, every team member earns reward_per_turn times the number of control points their team holds.

Rewards

  • reward_per_turn * num_flags_held per step, shared equally across team members.
  • Default: 10.0 / 512 per point per step, incentivizing sustained control.

Termination

Episode ends after length steps.
