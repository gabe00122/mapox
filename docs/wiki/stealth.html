Stealth

Asymmetric predator-prey environment. Sneakers (prey) gather food and hide in grass while Chasers (predators) hunt them down. Features grass concealment, food respawning, and a fullness cooldown mechanic.

Game Rules

Map Generation

  • Perlin noise generates permanent wall patterns (threshold > 0.1).
  • A second Perlin noise layer generates grass tiles (threshold < grass_threshold, default -0.5). Grass only appears on non-wall tiles.
  • Food is spawned on random open tiles (including grass).

Agent Roles

Sneakers (Prey)

  • Goal: collect food and avoid chasers.
  • Stepping onto a present food tile earns food_reward (default 1.0).
  • Grass concealment: sneakers standing on grass tiles are invisible to all agents' observations. The grass tile is shown instead of the agent.
  • When caught by a chaser, the sneaker loses catch_reward (default 1.0) and is respawned at a random open (non-grass) position.
  • Sneakers spawn on non-grass tiles.

Chasers (Predators)

  • Goal: catch sneakers.
  • A chaser catches a sneaker when they are adjacent (Manhattan distance ≤ 1), but only if the chaser is hungry (fullness = 0).
  • Catching a sneaker earns catch_reward and triggers a fullness cooldown (fullness_duration, default 5 steps) during which the chaser cannot catch again.
  • Chasers spawn on non-grass tiles.
Stealth mechanic: Sneakers on grass are invisible in observations, creating an information asymmetry. Chasers must infer sneaker positions from partial information, while sneakers can use grass strategically for cover.

Food System

  • num_food food items are placed at reset.
  • When eaten, a food tile enters a respawn timer (food_regrow_time, default 10 steps).
  • When the timer expires, the food respawns at a new random open position (including grass).
  • Multiple sneakers on the same food tile: the first (by index) gets the reward.

Actions Used

Primary action and dig are masked off.

Movement

  • Random execution order each step (via jax.lax.fori_loop).
  • Agents cannot move through walls or onto tiles occupied by another agent.
  • Agents can move onto grass tiles freely.

Catch Detection

  • Happens after all movement is resolved.
  • A sneaker is caught if any hungry chaser is within Manhattan distance 1 (including same tile).
  • Caught sneakers respawn immediately at a random non-grass position.

Rewards

AgentEventReward
SneakerEat food+food_reward (1.0)
SneakerCaught by chaser-catch_reward (-1.0)
ChaserCatch a sneaker+catch_reward (1.0)

Termination

Episode ends after length steps.
